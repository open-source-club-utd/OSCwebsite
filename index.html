About

Vision

To nurture a community around Learning and Creating

Mission

Most Freshmen and Sophomores have a hard time finding projects to put on their resumes.
And it can be intimidating to get started with building projects when its your first time.
We want to facilitate the process of starting off with development work,
by giving our members the skills and resources they need to get started!

Goals

For Fall 2019, we'll be focusing on Web Development
We've planned a series of workshops to cover both Front End and Back End development
For Front End, we'll be covering HTML+CSS, Javascript, Bootstrap and Semantic UI
For Back End, we'll primarily be using Node.js and Express.js
We'll also cover important tools and technologies relevant to web development
such as version control, API's, deployment etc.

Who is the club for?

Everyone, {student, faculty, staff}, is welcome at our events!
We especially encourage participation from freshmen and sophomore students!
